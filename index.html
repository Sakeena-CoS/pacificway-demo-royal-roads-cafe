Why We've Lasted 27 Years

Colwood's table since 1997.

At 1951 Sooke Road in Colwood, Royal Roads Cafe has been a cornerstone of the community for nearly three decades. Generations of families, students from Royal Roads University, and local workers have made us their regular spot — because we keep it real, keep it fresh, and keep showing up every day.

  • Homemade soups daily — made from scratch every morning, never from a can or a powder.
  • Fresh-baked goods — pies, muffins, and squares baked in our kitchen every morning.
  • Friendly neighbourhood service — we know our regulars by name and always make newcomers feel welcome.
  • Free parking — easy to get to and easy to park, right on Sooke Road.
  • Open early — we're up before you are, ready with coffee and a warm meal to start your day.
