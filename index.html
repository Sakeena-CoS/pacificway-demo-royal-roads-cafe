Our Story

Twenty-seven years and still going.

Royal Roads Cafe opened on Sooke Road in 1997 and has been a fixture of Colwood ever since. We've served Royal Roads students, families after soccer, retirees who've had the same table for a decade, and hikers who needed something real after the trail.

We're not trying to be anything other than what we are — a good neighbourhood cafe that takes care of the people who come through the door.
